Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

• To create an account, click on the 'Profile' link at the top right of the page.

• A dropdown menu will appear and an option to 'Register' will be there.

• Click on register and fill in the following information to create your account.

How can I find new music?

• Our website offers a 'Random' Feature that is located in the navigation bar at the top.

• This feature will give you a random song/album curated based on your interests.

• Our website also has a 'Search' function to search for specific songs/albums you are looking for.

How can I rate a song or album?

• To rate a song or album, first select an album that you would like to rate.

• You can select an album by clicking it, redirecting you to an album rating page where you can rate that album (on a scale from 1 to 5 stars).

• You are also able to leave a comment message that goes along with your rating.
